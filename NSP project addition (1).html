About

Poornima Institute of Engineering & Technology, was established in 2007 with the aim of imparting pragmatic technical education. In its magnificent journey of 12 years, PIET has set benchmarks and reached at new pinnacles in Engineering Education with dedication, perseverance and devotion. PIET is spearheading its outstanding voyage with the motto ‘Success is not a destination, it’s a journey’.

College Infrastructure

The college of Poornima institute of engineering and technology is well endorsed with facilities and necessities of its students. The college classrooms are very well equipped and developed.

Academics

The College's priority has always been the academic growth of a student. That's why more focus is laid on academics than any other activity. The College Management takes discipline very seriously. The teachers are very supportive and are quite unbiased when it comes to Exam marking pattern. The Academics also include NSPs to improve technical concepts of a student.
